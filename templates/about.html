Amanda Ford

Amanda Ford is a contemporary artist known for her use of colour and texture to influence her paintings. She uses various mediums including acrylic, watercolour, pastels and gouache. Her background includes studies in Design and Colour, and she ran her own Colour Consulting business for several years.
